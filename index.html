My name is Dorin Constantin Banu and this site is about my journey of self-taught web developement.
No, Im not a narcissist. Why do you ask?

I'm going to try and make this short and sweet. So I started with The Complete Web Developer in 2021: Zero to Mastery by Andrei Neagoie and The Web Developer Bootcamp 2021 by Colt Steele. And while their Udemy courses were good at explaining concepts, when I wanted to build a website from zero I draw a blank in my mind, I didn't know were to start and what to do.

After that experience I continued with the courses but felt kinda defeated but in my research I came across a website that claims to teach you real world coding and not just bits and pieces, and it was called The Odin Project. I saw that, compared to the Udemy courses, the Odin Project does not hold your hand, it show you how a concept is implemented and then it throws you in the ring to build with it, no hand holding, just coding.

While completing the lessons in The Odin Project it was obvious that I started to understand the concepts and if I was asked to build something I wasn't a chicken without a head, I knew what I had to do to finish the assigment that was given to me. At first it was hard, really hard, but I pushed through and gained knowledge and I trully understood that you learn to build websites by building them, not only going through tutorial after tutorial in an endless circle. What I learned so far, HTML5 CSS5 JavaScript Git and GitHub, I can confidently say that it was down to The Odin Project.

As I move forward and keep learning my current goal is to finish The Odin Project and then start learning WordPress and PHP8. It's going to be an interesting journey. I want to continue learning and become better at coding. Well, as I go through proof reading this it's blatantly obvious it wasn't that short as I stated at the start but I hope it was at least sweet.
